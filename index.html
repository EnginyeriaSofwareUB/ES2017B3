Welcome to Chicken Squads

About

Chicken Squads is a strategy videogame in turns, that takes you to a desert where chickens team up and shoot, bomb and hit each other while also destroying the arena where the slaughter happens.

  • Be the only one standing and win! - or earn a sufficient amount of points (Booooring)
  • Kill chickens like you’ve never killed
  • Have fun while bombing chickens of your team and the other
  • Destroy the arena like it was made of freakin’ paper

It's easy to play!

  • Move your chiken with the direction keys or A and D keys
  • Make a jump or a double jump with upper key or W
  • Select your weapon with 1, 2 or 3 keys and prepare for action
  • Aim with the mouse and shoot with it's left button to kill the chikens
  • If the scream of the chikens disturbs you, mute them and all sounds with M key
